What is Monster Master?

Monster Master is an expandable card game where you build a team of Monsters and Masters and fight with another team to see who's can their base the best. In the process, you can transform said characters into either weapons, evolutions, or even combine them to create a Monster Masters!

What makes Monster Master unique?

Monster Master mixes Tokusatsu Heroes, like Kamen Rider, with collectable monsters, like Pokemon; and not just that, it allows you to use those monsters as weapons, partners or even henshin suits for battle!

In terms of gameplay, this doesn't have a Resource System! Well, not like MTG at least, it's most similar to Yu Gi Oh! It also includes a Life Point System that gives you extra cards when you are losing, so comebacks are more possible!
